Schemes — browse & choose

Several cattle-induction schemes can run at once (different subsidy, animal, funding, eligibility). The member sees the list of schemes open at their society, opens one for details, and then checks eligibility / expresses interest for that specific scheme. Settled mock = the spec for GET /cattle-induction/schemes (list) + …/schemes/:version (one).

        Rules this mock encodes (→ backend spec):

        • Multiple schemes coexist — the list returns every scheme active for the member's society/district, each with its own rules_json. (New: the API moves from one "published" scheme to listSchemes + schemes/:version.)

        • Every term is config per scheme; each is versioned + immutable, and an application pins the scheme version it was submitted under.

        • Eligibility and EOI are scoped to one scheme — the chosen schemeVersion flows into GET …/eligibility?scheme= and POST …/interest.

        • A member may hold at most one open application per scheme; buffalo vs cattle caps are per-scheme config.

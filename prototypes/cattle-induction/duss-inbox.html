      Rules this mock encodes (→ backend spec):

      • Inbox = FORWARDED_TO_DUSS (own DUSS). Maker: scrutinise (→ UNDER_DUSS_SCRUTINY), raise deficiency (→ DOCUMENTS_INCOMPLETE, itemised, back to farmer).

      • Checker: generate bank batch — a bank-wise packet (content-addressed PDF/Excel, prescribed format) → each app SUBMITTED_TO_BANK.

      • Maker-checker segregation: the checker who generates a batch must differ from the maker who scrutinised (enforced server-side, 403 on violation).

      • Only verified applications can be batched; every action writes the domain_events outbox.

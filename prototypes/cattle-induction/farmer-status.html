        Rules this mock encodes (→ backend spec):
    
        • Timeline is derived from the append-only domain_events outbox (one row per transition), not a mutable log.
    
        • Every card carries an "as of" timestamp — screens show last-synced state honestly (offline-first).
    
        • A RETURNED_FOR_CORRECTION step shows the reason and the action the farmer must take; the clock restarts on the farmer.
    
        • Read is scoped to the owner (a farmer sees only their own application).
    
        • Money/purchase steps appear greyed as "future" in CIA-1 (they light up in CIA-2/3).

      Rules this mock encodes (→ backend spec):

      • Maker stages the file → rows parsed to MATCHED (found SUBMITTED_TO_BANK app) or UNMATCHED; dedup by file_row_hash (re-upload is idempotent).

      • Checker confirms → matched rows apply: SANCTIONED → LOAN_SANCTIONED, rejected → LOAN_REJECTED (reason); farmers intimated.

      • Unmatched rows are QUARANTINED — never auto-applied; resolved manually.

      • Maker-checker: the checker must differ from the maker who staged.

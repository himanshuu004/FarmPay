Allied KCC — Screen Wireframes

Every persona of the Cattle Induction (CIA) programme, screen by screen. Farmer (mobile app) and the field PWA (mobile · offline) render as phones; the back-office personas (DCS · DUSS · bank · UCDF) render as desktop web dashboards — the form-factor split is deliberate. Farmer screens mirror the built React Native / Expo app; field and back-office are settled prototypes.

★ = farmer-authored · ‡ = the CIA-only in-app exception (DCS & supervisor act in the app; routine co-op orders stay ERP-side). Form factor is deliberate: farmer + field are mobile (offline-first, camera/GPS); back-office is desktop web (bulk maker-checker, reconciliation tables). The seller-payment gate never opens until vet certificate + transit & cattle insurance + farmer acknowledgment + a complete traceability chain exist — and the app recommends, it never moves money.
